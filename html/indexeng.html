The ultimate Percy Jackson quiz

What can you expect from this quiz?


How well do you know the Percy Jackson universe? This quiz will take you from the cabins at Camp Half-Blood all the way to Olympus.

Here are some of the areas your knowlage will be challenged in:

  • Characters: Do you know your demigods, mortals, and monsters? Who belongs to which cabin? Who is the antagonist?
  • Plot and Prophecies: Do you remember the prophecy Percy received? Who does Luke help, and with what? What was stolen?
  • Mythology meets modern day: IIn the world of Percy Jackson, Greek myths blend with the modern world. Do you remember where the entrance to Olympus is? Or which labyrinth is especially important?
  • Magical Items: Riptide, the Helm of Darkness, the Master Bolt - magical artifacts play a big role. Do you remember who used what, and when?


Treat it like a quest. You may not have ambrosia or nectar on hand (though snacks never hurt), but you have your mind, your memory, and maybe a few satyrs cheering you on from the side. Trust your instincts. When in doubt, ask yourself: What would Annabeth do?


Remember: Just like in the books, there are many ways to be a hero. Maybe you recall every tiny detail about Annabeth’s growth. Or maybe you just love Greek mythology and enjoy catching all the references. Whatever your strength is – you’ve got what it takes to conquer this quiz.
